The Skredvarsel app is ready for its fifth season (2025/2026), with a major update.

- Swedish avalanche warnings from lavinprognoser.se
- Interactive map view showing all regions colored by danger level
- Map view on Apple Watch
- Swedish language support
- Siri Shortcuts: Ask Siri for the avalanche warning
- Updated for iOS 26 and watchOS 26
- New extra large widget size for iPad
- Avalanche problem graphics now show representative images
- Updated danger level colors to match the new official colors
- Emergency warning banner for critical alerts
- Share avalanche warnings as images via Messages, AirDrop, and more
- Favorite regions with iCloud sync across devices
- Offline support: cached data shows instantly on launch, works without network
- Improved reliability when returning to the app from the background
- Widgets and complications automatically refresh when the app is opened

Stay safe, and happy season!
